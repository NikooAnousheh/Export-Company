Song Of Africa is the Harmonious Blend of Flavor and Tradition!

Song of Africa is an export coffee company that is located in the heart of Africa, Kenya! In Kenya Coffee is the most important cash crop throughout all the years, making it the top export business earner since the 1980s. It has also specialized in the production of 2 types of Coffee. Kenya has always been famous for its Robusta coffee, an indigenous species that still grows wild in the country’s rainforests, you’ll also find Arabica growing in three regions: Mount Elgon in the east, the Rwenzori Mountains in the southwest, and West Nile in the northwest. Each origin is unique, with different coffee profiles and production methods. Each Coffee has its history in your specific region. We seek to ensure the access of small farmers to markets which is expected to witness significant growth owing to the increasing application of such beans in various sectors including pharmaceuticals, cosmetics, and food and beverages.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best way to store coffee beans?
Coffee beans should be stored in an airtight container in a cool, dark place to preserve their freshness and flavor.
How long do coffee beans last?
Coffee beans can last up to 6 months if stored properly, but for the best flavor, we recommend using them within 1-2 weeks of roasting.
What is the difference between Arabica and Robusta coffee beans?
Arabica beans are generally sweeter and more delicate, with flavors of sugar, fruit, and berries, while Robusta beans are stronger and more bitter, with a grain-like overtone. Robusta beans also contain twice as much caffeine as Arabica beans.
How long is the Lead-Time before my order is shipped?
Once order is confirmed with deposit paid, estimated turn-around lead time is between 10 – 25 days.
What Shipping options do I have?
